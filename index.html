The 12 Do's of Christmas*

*And Hanukkah, Kwanzaa, and every winter holiday people celebrate.

co: believes in doing. So to spread holiday cheer this year, we're taking our own advice, rolling up our sleeves, and choosing to do, not just donate.

The 12 Do's
(tap to do)
With the help of New York Cares, Project Art, and Brooklyn Community Service, we taught healthy eating to 1st and 2nd graders in the Rockaways, held coding workshops in a public school, giftwrapped toy donations, helped New York youth explore their creativity through art, celebrated birthdays at a Geriatric Psych Unit, coached kids 5-12 in creative writing, helped organize the New York Public Library, taught students creative cooking, inspired 5th graders to explore digital photography, helped Coney Island students ice skate, read bedtime stories to children in an East Harlem shelter, and gave a senior center a spa day.
